Schedule emails and text messages

You can schedule sending emails and text messages up to 4 days in advance.

To schedule a batch of messages:

  1. Go to the {{ service_link(current_service, 'main.choose_template', 'templates') }} page.
  2. Add a new template or choose an existing template and select Get ready to send.
  3. Upload a list or recipients.
  4. Set the date and time you want to send your messages.

Your scheduled messages will appear on the dashboard and uploads pages.
